Think-About Questions

  • How do you create students' self-awareness of a knowledge gap?
  • What are the steps for implementing the idea?
  • How will student learning be scaffolded?
  • What tasks will the teacher do, what will the students do?
  • What questions will engage students and provoke thinking?
  • What resources may be necessary for implementation?
