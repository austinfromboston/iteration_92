Break down a concept into parts and show relationships among the parts

When teaching...

  • Allow time for students to examine concepts and ideas and break them down into basic parts
  • Require students to explain why they chose a certain problem solving technique and why the solution worked
    Requries knowledge and comprehension- NOTE: application is NOT required-

Questioning Prompts

  • How can you classify according to ?
  • What explanation do you have for ?
  • How is connected to ?
  • Discuss the pros and cons of ?
  • How can you sort the parts of ?
  • What can you infer about ?
  • What ideas validate ?
  • What can you point out about ?
